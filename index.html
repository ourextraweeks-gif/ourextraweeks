How we ended up with too much of a good thing

Over the years, our family and a close group of friends got a little carried away buying timeshare weeks at some genuinely spectacular resorts: Westin Kaanapali on Maui, Westin Kierland in Scottsdale, and Garza Blanca in Puerto Vallarta, Cancun and Los Cabos. We love every one of them. The problem is, there are only so many weeks in a year.

Rather than let these weeks sit unused, we rent them out to fellow travellers who want a luxury resort experience without the ownership commitment. You get a stunning one or two bedroom villa at a top-tier resort; we get to feel slightly better about our timeshare habit. Everyone wins.

We handle the name-change paperwork and reservation transfer ourselves. No third-party broker fees, no markups. You're dealing directly with the owners, so communication is fast and personal.

Alternatively, you may continue to book via RedWeek, as we provide the RedWeek link for each listing (as soon as it's posted).

📋 How Direct Booking Works

Step 1
Express interest
Inquire via the form below, by email, or through RedWeek if a listing is posted there.
Step 2
Sign a contract
We'll send a rental agreement to sign. We'll need your full legal name, address as on your ID, email, and phone number, as the resort checks photo ID at check-in.
Step 3
Payment
A 50% deposit is due upon signing the contract. The remaining balance is due once the reservation is transferred into your name.
Step 4
Reservation transfer
We update the reservation into your name directly with the resort. You're all set!
Payment methods: Zelle, Interac e-Transfer (we'll provide the CAD equivalent), or PayPal Friends & Family. All amounts in USD. Any transaction fees are the responsibility of the payor.
Cancellation policy: All bookings are final. No cancellations or refunds once the reservation has been transferred.
Book via RedWeek: Guests are welcome to book through RedWeek if they prefer to pay by credit card or want the added security of a third-party platform. A RedWeek link is shown on each listing where available, and we update postings as we list.
Resellers welcome: Travel agents and resellers may purchase a week on behalf of a client. We'll hold the reservation and update the guest name once confirmed.
